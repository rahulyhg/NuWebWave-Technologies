Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) Process

Search engine optimization (SEO) process is a crucial step in taking the website on top on rakings and it has to be followed with the best ways. In seo process keywords and content study is a KEY. Always setting a targeted keywords, Targeted audience as well as content research are initial and the most important steps. Creating more unique content and we always have more chance of getting website on top rankings of search engines.

How the process of Search engine optimization (SEO) works :-

  • Competitive research of seo keywords
  • Setting a goal and target audience
  • Unique content building
  • Optimizing and indexing all pages on google and other search engines
  • Title of pages, Meta tags, Descriptions shall be used targeting the necessary keywords complimenting each other
  • Sitemap building
  • Social media link creation and connecting it to website and being active on social media
  • Robot txt building
  • Reporting and analysis of the work done as per target
