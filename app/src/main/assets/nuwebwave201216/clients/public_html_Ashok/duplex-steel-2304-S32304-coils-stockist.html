Alloy 2304

Ashok Metal Corporation are leading manufacturer, stockist & suppliers of Duplex Steel 2304 Plates UNS S32304 as per the International Standards. These Duplex Steel 2304 Plates are produced from high quality raw materials which ensures the International Standards of the product.

Alloy 2304 is a 23% chromium, 4% nickel, without molybdenum duplex stainless steel. The Alloy 2304 has erosion resistance properties like 316L. Besides, its mechanical properties, i.e., yield quality, are twice those of 304/316 austenitic evaluations. This permits the architect to spare weight, especially for legitimately planned weight vessel applications. The alloy is especially appropriate for applications covering the - 50°C/+300°C (- 58°F/572°F) temperature go. Bring down temperatures may likewise be considered however require a few confinements, especially for welded structures.
