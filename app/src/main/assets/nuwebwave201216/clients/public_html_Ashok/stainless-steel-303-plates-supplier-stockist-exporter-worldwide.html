Alloy 303

Ashok Metal Corporation are leading exporter, stockist & suppliers of Stainless Steel 303 Plates UNS S30300 Werkstoff 2.4660 as per the International Standards. These Stainless Steel 303 Plates are produced from high quality raw materials which ensures the International Standards of the product.

Alloy 303 (UNS S30300) is a non-magnetic, austenitic, free-machining stainless steel that is an adjustment of the fundamental 10% chromium - 8% nickel stainless steel exceptionally intended to display enhanced machinability while keeping up great mechanical and consumption safe properties.
