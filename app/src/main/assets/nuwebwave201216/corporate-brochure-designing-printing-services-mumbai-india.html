Nuwebwave Technologies Provided Different type of Brochure designing:-

  • Catalogue:- Catalogue Whenever you go for your important meetings and conferences, imagine your store gallery being held in the hands of your clients, it’s called a catalogue. They are important and excellent impression for your buyers. Your buyers will be excelled to see your products or services in detail & to check all that you offer. Catalogs come in various sizes and formats, but they usually have a minimum of 8 pages. The maximum depends on the way you want your catalog to be.
  • Bi-fold brochures:- A bi-fold brochure is a 4-panels piece formed from folding a quality double-LTR sheet of paper. You can stack and staple bi-fold brochures to obtain brochures with more pages, always multiple of 4 you can get a 4-page bi-fold, an 8-page bi-fold, a 12-page bi-fold and so on. Bi-fold brochures are pieces used by companies to introduce themselves, present their products and services, and to accomplish other marketing and promotional objectives. As such, the outer panels must enjoy a striking and eye-catching design so as to invite the reader to open the brochure and read the content that is found on the inner panels. As they are generally printed on quality paper, they are very durable .A bi-fold that is professionally designed, and that is printed on high-quality paper will be highly attractive and will be an excellent tool for your marketing efforts, showing your customers your professionalism and dedication.
  • Tri-fold brochures:- Tri-folds are brochures generally made of LTR or LGL sheets of paper folded in 3. Printed on both sides, they result in 6 panels. Compare to bifolds, tri-folds have more panels available for content, and are best-suited for less formal marketing campaigns. They are a better alternative to flyers as they are much more elegant and attractive.
  • Pamphlet designing:- A pamphlet is a one sheet consisting of a printed paper -usually one page that with an unbound paper cover- which informs of a subject of your company promotion, offers, notices, discounts etc. Pamphlets are small in size, but large in Impact for selling your goods and services in your locality as they bring sense of knowingness to others what you have in stock, what you are providing, any discounting offers etc. The aim is to achieve mass distribution so that you get direct and fast sales.
  • Flyers:- Flyers are generally one-sheet pamphlets intended for mass distribution. They are printed on low-quality paper because they are not intended to have a long-lasting life. Their general aim is to promote offers. They are small in size - they are generally LTR sized or smaller. Flyers are very common pieces. Almost every shop produces a large print-run of flyers per year to promote their products. For this reason, it's very important that your flyer stands out from the rest.
  • Folders:- Folders are basically bifolds, usually with flaps added, whose main functionalities are to protect inserts and to provide a touch of elegancy. Promotional material delivered inside high-quality folders will be much more efficient than loose sheets of paper. However, if the design of your folder is poor in terms of marketing strategies and graphic design aspects, readers will reject it and never get to know the material your offer.
